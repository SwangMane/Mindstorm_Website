About Mindstorm

Our community

Mindstorm is a server hosting group created by a few long-time friends aimed at providing a free-to-play, community-driven environment on a variety of different games.

Mindstorm origins

Part-owners DARKminerKS and Blockk met online in early 2013 while playing on the same public Minecraft server. The game was then just recently updated to 1.6, known as the 'Horse update'. The pair, along with a few others kept in touch and eventually started up a server of their own. Part-Owners Pudding and HillbillyDeluex joined Mindstorm sometime in the year 2014.
